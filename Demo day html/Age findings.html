Injuries by Player Age

We found that the greatest overall frequency of player injury typically occurred around the age of 27, however the average age of an NBA player in our data set is 26.5; most players in our data set are 24 to 29 years old. Note: To make the histograms easier to read we applied bins to player ages, so graphed values may not line up with stated player ages by injury type.
